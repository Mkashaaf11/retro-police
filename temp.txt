Sample Usage for Color Coding
Primary Background: Navy Blue or Dark Gray
Headers/Navigation: A darker blue or black for a professional look.
Text: Light gray or white for readability.
Primary Buttons: Use brighter blue or red (depending on the tone you want for urgency).
Accent Buttons: Gold/yellow for standout actions like “Submit” or “Finish.”
Icons: Match icons to background for contrast; use white on dark backgrounds and black or blue on light backgrounds.
Recommended Color Palette
Primary Blue: #1A237E or #0D47A1
Dark Gray/Black: #212121 or #000000
White (for text): #FFFFFF
Accent Gold/Yellow: #FFC107
Red (for alerts): #D32F2F
